Research Background

Why Gap Reduction is Essential Beyond Traditional Career Support

The "Initiative" Perception Gap

Student Mindset: Self-Growth Orientation

"Proactive learning attitude", "Developing personal skills", and "Researching without waiting for explicit instructions."

Corporate Expectation: Organizational Contribution

"Identifying and autonomously solving team problems", and "Engaging stakeholders to create tangible value."

While universities offer rich learning opportunities (Active Learning, Internships), the key bottleneck is that students lack clarity on "which concrete actions equal corporate-level initiative."
Platform Concept

Evolution of AKINAI: From Knowing to Acting

1 Know : Assess your current skill radar
2 Understand : Recognize gaps against industry standards
3 Act (AI Challenge) : Execute personalized micro-actions
4 Reduce the Gap : Bridge initiative gap for real-world growth
AI does not unilaterally score or grade students; it serves as a continuous 24/7 personal coach guiding them step-by-step.
